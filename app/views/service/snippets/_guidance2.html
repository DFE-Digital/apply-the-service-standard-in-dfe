You should consider the [Service Standard points for discoveries](link to /service-assurance/prepare-for-discovery-peer-review) for your project. The [GOV.UK Service Standard](https://apply-the-service-standard.education.gov.uk/service-standard) helps you to build accessible, usable services and guidance.
